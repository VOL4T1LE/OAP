
##########################################
Nerve Agent Exposure Mnemonic | Memory Aid
##########################################

SLUDGEM, a mnemonic for the indications of
exposure to a nerve agent.

S  |  Salivation (Drooling)
L  |  Lacrimation (Tearing up)
U  |  Urination
D  |  Defecation (Excretion)
G  |  Gastrointestinal Upset
E  |  Emesis (Vomiting)
M  |  Miosis (Pupillary constriction)
