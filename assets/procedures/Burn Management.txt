BURN MANAGEMENT
 > Assess and note the TBSA percentage covered in burns
 > Irrigate the burns with Normal Saline Solution
 > Dress the burns with polyvinyl chloride dressings
 > Consider the necessity of administering Morphine Sulphate
 > Consider the necessity of Oxygen Therapy in dyspnoea
 > Consider the necessity of a Chest Escharotomy in cases of dyspnoea or apnoea
