The 5 P's

  P  |  Pulse (Assess patients' pulse's consistency around the wound)
  P  |  Perfusion (DCRT, Pallor / Cyanosis, etc)
  P  |  Paraesthesia (Numbness, tingling, or burning sensation)
  P  |  Paralysis (Inability to move the limb)
  P  |  Pain (Assess with patient pain assessment)
