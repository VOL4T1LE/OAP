BURN MANAGEMENT
 > Assess and note the TBSA percentage affected by burns
 > Note the type of burns present
 > Irrigate the burns with Normal Saline Solution
 > Dress the burns with polyvinyl chloride dressings
 > Consider the necessity of administering Morphine Sulphate for analgesia
 > Consider the necessity of Oxygen Therapy in cases of dyspnoea or hypoxia
 > Consider the necessity of a Chest Escharotomy in cases of dyspnoea or apnoea
