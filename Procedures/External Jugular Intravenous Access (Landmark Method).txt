PREPARATION
 *I'd don a clean pair of nitrile gloves*
 *I'd tilt the patient's head 50 degrees to one side*
 > The patient's head should be tilted away from the desired insertion site / side
 *I'd locate the EJV running over the posterior border of the stemocleidomastoid muscle*
 *I'd sterilise the site with a 2% chlorhexidine wipe*
 *I'd allow the site to dry*
 *I'd get a 20g catheter*
 *I'd remove the needle safety cap and discard it*
 *I'd ensure the catheter is seated back in the notch*
 *I'd get 5mL of 1% Lidocaine Hydrochloride in a 5mL syringe*
 *I'd locally infiltrate 5mL of 1% Lidocaine Hydrochloride*

EJIV LINE PLACEMENT
 *I'd stabilise the vein and place my thumb above the PT's clavicle*
 *I'd maintain skin traction*
 *I'd insert the needle, midway between the angle of the jaw and the clavicle*
 *I'd await flashback in the flash chamber*
 *Upon flashback, I'd insert a guidewire through the needle*
 *I'd retract the needle and discard it*
 *I'd insert the catheter over the guidewire*
 *I'd secure the catheter with some adhesive tape*
 *I'd connect an extension set to the catheter*
 *I'd place a Tegaderm over the site of the EJIV catheter*
